What We Offer

Services under one roof

We believe in integrated healthcare — having your GP, specialist, and allied health providers all in one place means better-coordinated care and fewer gaps.

General Consultations

Comprehensive care for acute illness, chronic disease management, and preventive health for all ages.

Family & Child Health

Dedicated care for growing families including immunisations, developmental checks, and paediatric consultations.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing management plans for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and other chronic conditions.

Mental Health

Compassionate support including Mental Health Care Plans, counselling referrals, and ongoing reviews.

Women's Health

Cervical screening, contraception, pregnancy care, menopause management, and breast health assessments.

Skin Checks & Procedures

Skin cancer checks, mole mapping, and minor surgical procedures performed on-site by experienced GPs.

About the Practice

Where patients come first

Currambine Family Practice is an independent, doctor owned medical practice established in 2010. We provide quality primary health care in the northern suburbs and are unwavering in our commitment to excellence in ethical and clinical standards in general practice.

At Currambine Family Practice, our mission is to provide exceptional, comprehensive and personalised health care to our community. We strive to build lasting relationships with you and your family by offering the highest standard of clinical care and advice with compassion and empathy.
