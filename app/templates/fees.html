Billing Information

Billing & Medicare

After Hours & Saturday Fees

All appointments scheduled before 8am or after 5pm and on Saturdays will be privately billed. This includes children, pensioners and health care card holders.

Telehealth Appointments

Telehealth appointments are privately billed. Children and pensioners may be bulk billed by selected doctors. Please check with our reception team when you book.

Health Care Card Holders

Health Care Card holders will be privately billed at a reduced rate. Bulk billing may be available — please discuss this with your doctor.

Pension Card Holders & Children Under 12

Current pension cardholders and children under 12 years old may be bulk billed by selected doctors during regular office hours.

Medicare Claims

We can lodge your Medicare claim on your behalf electronically immediately after your appointment. In most cases Medicare will pay the rebate into your account by the end of the next business day.

Cancellations

Please advise us if you need to cancel your appointment as soon as possible so that we can care for someone else. We may charge a fee if you repeatedly fail to attend for your appointment.
