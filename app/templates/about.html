About the Practice

About us

Currambine Family Practice is an independent, doctor owned medical practice established in 2010. We provide quality primary health care in the northern suburbs and are unwavering in our commitment to excellence in ethical and clinical standards in general practice.

Your wellbeing is our priority

Our team of doctors, allied health professionals, nurses and administrative staff work collaboratively to ensure that patient care is comprehensive and holistic.

Personalised care

We prioritise your health journey by catering to your individual needs and providing the best experience for you and your family.

Taking care of the community

We will always make every attempt to see emergencies, sick children and the elderly.

Education & Research

Training future doctors and research

We are dedicated to training the next generation of general practitioners and are actively involved in general practice research.
